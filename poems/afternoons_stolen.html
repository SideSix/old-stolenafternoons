Afternoons Stolen

I watch Marx Brothers films waiting
for the scene where Harpo steals
a few moments to tilt
a harp between his legs
His fingers pluck strings
tenderly, at first, caressing
flowing faster to a crescendo
his fingers gliding, yielding
circles of rushing harmonic sound
I wonder what the air feels like
between his fingers and the strings
vibrating, changing that funny man’s face
making it even more tender
a child-like focus as he
and the harp merge into something
neither m had planned.
When I’m in bed with you
afternoons stolen in consecrated motel rooms
like Harpo Marx I become lost
in that space between strings and fingers.
